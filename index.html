About marksheet

A marksheet system records and manages students’ academic performance. It includes details like subjects, marks, total, percentage, and grades. Digital systems automate calculations, reduce errors, and save time. They store data securely and help in generating results quickly, making academic record management efficient, accurate, and easy to access.
